About Greg

Greg moved to Steubenville in 2014 to study at Franciscan University, and quickly fell in love with the city and the community that surrounded it. He began volunteering for a local nonprofit, The Harmonium Project, to put on concerts, plays, and festivals in efforts to revitalize Steubenville’s downtown. After graduating with a bachelor's degree from Franciscan University in 2018, he received his real estate license from Hondros College. He lives in Steubenville with his wife, Emily, and daughter, Mabel.
